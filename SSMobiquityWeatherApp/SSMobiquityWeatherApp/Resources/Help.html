How to use::

SSMobiquity Weather App gives you the live weather information(Temeprature, Humidity, Pressure, Wind speed...etc) based on the city you have picked.

Features:

1. User can bookmark any location by long pressing on Map shown.

2. User can also bookmark multiple location on map.

3. On selecting any city under the list shown, User will navigate to City Details Screen.

4. On Navigating to City Details Screen, user can view Today's temperature, humidity and wind speed details.

5. Able to delete the bookmarked city by tapping on 'X' buttom right to each city.
